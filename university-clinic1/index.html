FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book an appointment?

Simply sign up or log in to your account, navigate to your dashboard, and click on 'Schedule Appointment'. Choose your preferred date and time, and you'll receive a confirmation.

Is the online consultation service free?

Yes! All enrolled university students have free access to our online consultation services as part of their student health benefits.

How is my personal health information protected?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized healthcare professionals.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule appointments up to 2 hours before the scheduled time through your patient dashboard.
